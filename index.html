FAQ

What games do you support?

We support a wide range of popular and indie games, with more added regularly. If a game matters to you, chances are we can help with it.

Are guides really personalized?

Yes—everything is tailored to your experience level, progress, and preferred playstyle. You won’t get recycled or generic walkthroughs.

Do I need to be good at the game already?

Not at all. Achievement Atlas is built for all skill levels, from casual players to hardcore completionists.

How does live assist work?

A professional joins you via voice call and, when applicable, directly in-game. They guide you in real time, helping you overcome challenges efficiently and confidently.

Can I use more than one service?

Absolutely. Many players combine guides, coaching, and live assist for the best possible experience.

Do you offer refunds if I can't be helped?

We do not offer refunds. However, you can write an appeal if a professional was no cooperative, and if it is accepted, we will offer free services until you get what you originally paid for.
